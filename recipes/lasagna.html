Lasagna

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. A more traditional homemade lasagna filling would be made with ricotta but my mom's recipe calls for a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan. I have found none better anywhere. Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Canned Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Cottage
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Eggs

Recipe

  1. Cook the meat in a skillet until browned.
  2. Add onion and cook until transluscent.
  3. Stir in the tomato sace.
  4. Add herbs.
  5. Boil noodle till al dente.
  6. Make cheese layer by mixing cottage cheese, parmesan cheese, and egg.
  7. Coat the pan in sauce then place noodle on it followed by cheese
  8. Keep layering ingredients until out of noodle.
  9. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top noodle layer.
  10. Bake lasagna at 375 degrees for 50 minutes.
